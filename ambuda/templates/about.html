About

Our mailing list shares regular updates on Ambuda and our other Sanskrit projects. Our code is all on GitHub, and we eagerly welcome contributors of all backgrounds. For all other inquiries, please reach out to us directly.

Almost all of Ambuda's data comes from other projects.

Text data mostly comes from GRETIL. For the Ramayana and Mahabharata specifically, we instead use the "official" electronic versions hosted on John D. Smith's website. As our project grows, we will include other sources of textual data and even generate texts on our own.

Dictionary data comes from the Cologne Digital Sanskrit Lexicons (CDSL) project.

Code

All of our code is available on GitHub. We use Python (Flask) for the backend, Tailwind for CSS, and native JavaScript for the rest. Our site is hosted on DigitalOcean.
